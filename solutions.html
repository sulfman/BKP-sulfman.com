• Ensure all resources are accessed securely, regardless of location.

• Inspect and log all traffic.
• Strengthened security and streamlined automation.

Fraud Protection

A risk-based approach that protects users through every phase of the fraud lifecycle is the strongest approach to prevent targeted fraud.
Benefits of Fraud Protection
• Provide comprehensive fraud protection across the fraud lifecycle.
• Frictionless authentication using threat intelligence.
• Remove threats targeting your users.
• Detailed reporting on which users were victimized.
• Machine Learning adapts to user behavior and detects unusual activity in real time.
Why AppGate’s Fraud Protection
• Appgate’s Digital Threat Protection and Risk Based Authentication provide a layered protection strategy across the multi-step fraud lifecycle.
• As fraudsters target users through phishing, social media, rogue mobile apps and more, Appgate Digital Threat Protection proactively detects and removes these attempts to harvest valuable credentials. Preventing fraud early in the lifecycle minimizes the impact to your customers.
• As most attacker’s harvest information, users inadvertently click on a link or download something malicious onto their device. You cannot control your consumers’ device, but with Appgate Digital Threat Protection and Risk Based Authentication you can obtain device posture visibility to manage device risk prior and take the appropriate course of remediation.
• As fraudsters have successfully collected credentials, or any sensitive information, they can access a user’s account to steal money. Appgate Risk Based Authentication uses behavioral analytics and strong authentication to stop an attack before reaching the final stage of the fraud lifecycle.

Email Security

Emails are the most convenient channels of communication in today’s digital age. Almost every computer literate person has either sent or received an email. The disadvantage of business emails is that hackers use this medium to send messages with malicious content to steal information and cause financial loss. Business organizations can adopt Domain-based message authentication, reporting, and conformance (DMARC) solution to counter this threat from cybercriminals.
Benefits of DMARC Right:
• DMARC provides full insight in business email channels and makes phishing attacks visible.
• DMARC is more powerful: DMARC is capable of mitigating the impact of phishing and malware attacks.
• DMARC prevents email spoofing, protects against Brand Abuse, scams and avoid Business Email Compromise (BEC)
• DMARC prevents employees from spear phishing and CEO fraud attacks
Why choose DMARC Right for your email security:
• DMARC Right is cloud-based and uses Message Transfer Agents (MTAs)
• DMARC Right ensures prevention of revenue loss and damaged brand reputation.
• DMARC Right protects employees from spam and sophisticated fraud attempts by prohibiting unauthorized use of email domain.
• DMARC Right provides insightful overview on who is sending emails over the internet using the organization email domain.
• DMARC Right makes it easy to identify emails among a huge number of DMARC enabled recipients.

Phishing Simulator

In today’s digital age, social engineering attacks are prevalent and increasing. The human layer is often ignored and is generally the weakest link among any organizational cyber security apparatus. Attackers know this and exploit it. More than 91% of successful data breaches started with a spear phishing attack. With a phishing simulator, organizations can easily conduct simulated phishing attacks to test employees and train employees.
Benefits of Phishing Simulator:
• Phishing simulator allows for the creation of custom phishing campaigns with many customized phishing templates, targeted at different employees and groups within the organization.
