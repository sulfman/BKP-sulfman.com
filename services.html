ITIL
The IT Infrastructure Library is a framework of best practices which elaborates on the disciplines for IT Service Management. It covers important best practices in the areas of Service Delivery, Service Support, ICT Infrastructure Management, Security Management, Business Perspective, Application Management, Software Asset Management, Implementing Service Management, ITIL Small-scale operation.
ISO 20000
ISO 20000 is the international standard for IT Service Management which supersedes the British standard, BS 15000. It covers different domains such as Service Delivery Processes and Control processes which include Release, Resolution, and Relationship Management. It also specifies distinct management requirements and the maintenance of Service Management Policies and Plans, Service Level Agreements, and relevant records. This standard was developed in line with IT infrastructure library (ITIL) framework.
Why implement ISO 20000
Provides a platform to organize current IT services into formal framework
Gives assurance to stakeholders and clients for management of IT services
Align and integrate IT services with overall business strategy
Provides a process for Information Technology Governance
Increase overall organization IT operational performance
ISO 20000 certification is wide-reaching and recognized globally
Healthier market outcome due to positive influence on corporate brand

COBIT

COBIT which stands for Control Objectives for Information and related Technology, is a comprehensive framework of interest and relevance to all IT stake holders. Businesses worldwide have adopted the framework to align their IT objectives with the business objectives thus being able to implement IT Governance initiatives. COBIT is drafted and released by ITGI - a body of international experts in the domain.

• COBIT helps save resources through process, application, people and infrastructure alignment.
• COBIT was founded with an audit focus and has evolved to an IT Governance focus – the framework thus is the best representation of the evolving best IT practices.
• COBIT helps with linking specific IT Practices to overall business control models.
• COBIT identify meaningful measures of IT implementations, thus enabling senior management to measure return on investment on IT spending.

The standard points to all information assets in an organization irrespective of where it is stored or located. Sulfman Consulting provides ISO 27001:2013 full implementation and certification.
ISO 27001 has 11 domain areas, 39 control objectives and 133 controls in all. The security controls represent information security best practices, and the standard suggests that these controls should be applied depending on the business requirements.

ISO 31000

ISO 31000 is an international standard that provides guidelines on managing any type of risk in any business activity. The standard provides guidelines on principles, risk management framework, and application of the risk management process.
ISO 31000 is applicable to organizations of all types and sizes who seek to integrate risk management into business functions. It covers the risk management principles which are the foundation for managing risk, and guides organizations in developing a risk management framework.
Why implement ISO 31000
• ISO 31000 provides a level of assurance that allows organizations to succeed and thrive in an environment of constant change.
• ISO 31000 improves operational efficiency by facilitating the integration of risk-based decision-making into governance, planning, management, reporting, policies, values, and culture of an organization.
• ISO 31000 enables organizations to identify the potential risks that could hinder the achievement of business objectives.
• ISO 31000 helps organizations to determine the significance of risks and decide which risks should be mitigated first to achieve the objectives before they affect the business, and effectively keep all other risks under control.
• ISO 31000 best practices allow organizations to develop a desired risk management culture.
Why choose us for your ISO 31000 implementation
• Our approach ensures your organization follows the legal, regulatory, and statutory requirements.
• We create awareness of risk management principles and other underlying concepts of risk.
• We develop a risk management framework that is tailored to the needs and context of the organization.
• We create awareness of the importance of integrating risk management into significant activities and functions of the organization.
• We demonstrate commitment and competence throughout the implementation process.
Our approach for ISO 31000 implementation
• Identify threats that could affect operations and assets.
• Estimate the likelihood of such threats based on historical information and judgment of knowledgeable individuals.
• Rank threats based on value, sensitivity, and criticality to the operations and assets to determine which are the most important.
• Estimate the potential losses or damage that could occur if a threat materializes, including recovery costs.
• Identify cost-effective actions to mitigate or reduce the risk.
• Develop an action plan.
